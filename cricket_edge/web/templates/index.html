System design

What this is

A local-first cricket betting research system that separates two layers by design: statistical models estimate probability, fair odds, and edge; hard risk rules then decide whether execution is allowed and at what stake. The broker is paper-only until the models prove calibration and closing-line value over a meaningful sample.
